Questions and Answers

How far in advance do I need to place my order?
We highly recommend placing orders at least 3 - 4 weeks in advance for any special order as our schedule does book up. We do not guarantee availability until an order has been confirmed.
Do you sell vegan cakes?
Yes, we have variety of vegan cakes and baked goods. Please contact us if you have specific requests for ingredients.
How long does a cake stay fresh?
As a general rule, our cakes stay fresh at room temperature up to one week.
